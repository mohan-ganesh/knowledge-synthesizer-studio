- `knowledge-synthesizer-studio` is a sub project that is a knowledge synthesizer studio. It includes two standalone microservices `server-api` is a backend service and `web` is a frontend service. The backend service is hosted on CloudRun at https://knowledge-base-api-964252795915.us-central1.run.app and the frontend service is hosted on AppEngine Standard and accessible at https://mohan-ganesh.appspot.com/knowledge-synthesizer/ as a sub path/module
